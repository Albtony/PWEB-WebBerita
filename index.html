Pokemon Fans Designs Fire-Type Mudkip

A Pokemon player has created a Fire-Type version of the starter pocket monster Mudkip. Gamers like to take Game Freak's various creatures and give them new designs based on alternate Typings. This has led to many interesting Type changes for various Pokemon, as players imagine what could be.

Fan Plans Sweet Animal Crossing-Themed Birthday Party For Wife

To celebrate the birthday of their significant other, a fan of Animal Crossing recently planned a themed birthday party using popular elements found in the life sim series. Given that Nintendo's Animal Crossing series has been released through several decades and across different gaming consoles, it is not surprising for players to still be fans despite growing up and being in different stages of their lives.

Elden Ring: How to Get to Radahn

Starscourge Radahn is a Shardbearer in Elden Ring and defeating him is necessary to advance in some quests in the Lands Between. Reaching Radahn can be tricky as the boss is located in Redmane Castle, Caelid. It's a heavily protected Castle that requires special methods to enter it, like the Radahn Festival.
